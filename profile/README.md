# TransferTrack

TransferTrack helps community college students make sense of the transfer process.

A lot of students are trying to figure out what classes they need, which schools they can realistically transfer to, what deadlines matter, how much it will cost, and what they should be doing each semester. That information is usually spread across different college websites, PDFs, advisor notes, and portals.

Our goal is simple: make transferring less confusing. TransferTrack brings the important parts of the process into one place so students can plan their path, track requirements, compare schools, and feel more confident about what comes next.
